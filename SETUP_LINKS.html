⏳ Phase 2: Get Credentials Now (Open These Links)

1Get APPLE_ID_PASSWORD

  1. Click the button below to open Apple ID Management
  2. Sign in with: admin@garageos.co
  3. Click "Security" in the sidebar
  4. Click "App-specific passwords"
  5. Click "Generate password"
  6. Select "GitHub Actions" as the app
  7. Click "Generate"
  8. COPY THE PASSWORD IMMEDIATELY (format: xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx)
  9. Save it somewhere safe - it only shows once!
📱 Open Apple ID Management
⚠️ Important: The password will only display once. Copy it immediately and save it securely.

2Get APPLE_TEAM_ID

  1. Click the button below to open Apple Developer Account
  2. Sign in with: admin@garageos.co
  3. Click "Membership" in the sidebar
  4. Look for "Team ID" field (10 characters like: ABCD1234EF)
  5. COPY THE TEAM ID
👨‍💻 Open Apple Developer Account

3Create App Store Connect App & Get ASC_APP_ID

  1. Click the button below to open App Store Connect
  2. Sign in with: admin@garageos.co
  3. Click "Apps" in the sidebar
  4. Click the "+" button to create a new app
  5. Fill in the form:
    • Platform: iOS
    • Name: Garage OS
    • Primary Language: English
    • Bundle ID: com.garageos.mobile
    • SKU: garageos-mobile
  6. Click "Create"
  7. Click "App Information" in the sidebar
  8. Find the "Apple ID" field (a number like: 1234567890)
  9. COPY THE APPLE ID NUMBER (NOT the Bundle ID)
🏪 Open App Store Connect
⚠️ Important: Copy the Apple ID NUMBER, not the Bundle ID. They look different!
