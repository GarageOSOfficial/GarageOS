# Garage OS™ Roadmap

Strategic product roadmap organized into three phases: NOW (current), NEXT (planned), and LATER (future vision).

Does not include dates. Prioritization within each phase is flexible based on builder feedback and technical dependencies.

---

## NOW

### v0.3 Vehicle Workspace

Center of the application. The Vehicle Workspace is where builders manage all vehicle information, activities, and progress.

**Focus:** Core workspace management system providing the organizational hub for all vehicle-related work.

- Vehicle profile and configuration
- Workspace dashboard and layout
- Navigation and organizational structure
- Activity feed integration
- Vehicle data display and management

**Status:** In Development

---

## NEXT

### v0.4 Timeline

Comprehensive timeline view showing vehicle history and progress chronologically.

**Focus:** Visual representation of all activities and milestones related to a vehicle.

### v0.5 Inventory

Vehicle parts and component tracking system.

**Focus:** Track purchased and installed parts, organize inventory, and manage component history.

### v0.6 Budget

Financial planning and cost tracking for maintenance and improvements.

**Focus:** Budget planning, cost tracking, and financial analysis for vehicle projects.

### v0.7 Records

Document and service record management and archival.

**Focus:** Store, organize, and retrieve maintenance records, service history, and documentation.

### v0.8 Build Summary™

Overview and documentation of vehicle build configuration and complete history.

**Focus:** Comprehensive build documentation, configuration tracking, and progress visibility.

---

## LATER

### Internal Alpha

Hardened internal release for team testing and validation before broader release.

**Focus:** Production-ready internal version with comprehensive testing and quality assurance.

### Plank Owner Beta

Beta program for early adopters and key stakeholders providing real-world validation.

**Focus:** Limited beta release to gather feedback and validate product-market fit.

### Public Launch

Full public release of Garage OS for general availability.

**Focus:** Production deployment to public market with complete documentation and support.
